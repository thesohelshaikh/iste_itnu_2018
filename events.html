Freshers' Frenzy

Adding a ring to an already long chain of events, last year, ISTE Students' Chapter, Nirma University had engineered a fresh series of events for the eight hundred freshers' who came abroad the journey of engineering this year.

ISTE-MUN

The two day event saw numerous national and international delegates thronging the campus and addressing current world issues related to regional conflicts, peacekeeping, human rights, women and children, economic and social development, environment etc. The event ended with delegates reflecting their day on themselves through their communication skills. ISTE-MUN turned out to be a prestigious learning experience for both, the participants as well as the organisers and is another feather in the cap of ISTE which has yet again proven its metal by organising such an esteemed and large-scale event, successfully, in the university campus.

Freshers' Talent

Awaited by all, Freshers’ Talent is chapter’s trademark event which received amazing response, with audience applauses echoing the auditorium. This event witnessed amazing innate talents in terms of dancing, singing, mimicry, instument playing, etc of the new entrants in college hosted by amazing anchors from within the campus. To adjunicate the participants and to add to the charm, we had among us Youtube fame Nikita Ahuja, Murga King RJ Kunal and India’s got talent fame; the warriors crew as judges. After many spell bounding performances, Pankti Bardolia, from CE won the hearts and trophy of Ms. Fresher with her memorable solo drama act on ‘bodki’. On the other hand Munignya Patel, from mechanical enthralled the audience with his fantastic dance performance, bagging the title of Mr.Fresher.
